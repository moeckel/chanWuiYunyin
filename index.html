Chan Wui & Yunyin Rising Star Workshop

The Analytical Relationship Between
Mobility and Communications

The Chan Wui & Yunyin Rising Star Workshop is convening an elite group of academics to explore the nexus of mobility and communications.

The workshop has two main goals:

1. In-depth discussions between the selected senior fellows and fellows about the critical research questions stemming from changes in transportation and telecommunications and from the intersections of the two fields; and
2. Personalized advice and guidance from the senior fellows to the fellows on the skills essential for success in academic careers.

6th to 13th of July 2016, Queen Mary 2
